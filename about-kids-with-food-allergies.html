Kids With Food Allergies (KFA): Keeping Children With Food Allergies Safe and Healthy


KFA's mission:
We improve the day-to-day lives of families raising children with food allergies and empower them to create a safe and healthy future for their children.

We raise funds to support programs that include practical management and day-do-day support. We want families to not live in fear and to raise healthy, confident children who can learn to manage their own food allergies as they grow up.

We are dedicated to offering all tools, recipes and educational resources completely free of charge. We want those who need credible, valuable information to have access to it regardless of their financial means. We thank our donors, grantors and corporate partners for making this possible.


We have three major programming areas:

  • Education – We cover everything parents need to know from cradle to sending their children off to college.  We have many free resources available on our website - and you can also sign up for our e-newsletters.

  • Peer Support – We know that managing a child’s life-threatening food allergies can be isolating. Our online support forums – staffed by parent volunteers – help by providing peer-to-peer support, information sharing, and help with safe recipes, baking and cooking.

  • Food and Cooking – Whether they’re allergic to one food or a long list of foods, children with food allergies still need to eat. Our fabulous Safe Eats™ Recipe Collection offers 1,200 recipes searchable by whatever allergens a child needs to avoid.
